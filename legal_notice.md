# Legal Notice and Ethical Use

This project was built **strictly for educational purposes**, as part of a hands-on study in offensive security and penetration testing.

## Permitted use

- WiFi networks you own
- Networks with explicit, written authorization from the responsible party
- Dedicated lab environments (e.g. your own router set up for practice)
- Legal training platforms (HackTheBox, TryHackMe, DVWA, etc. — where applicable)

## Prohibited use

- Testing, capturing, or attempting to access third-party networks without authorization
- Any use that violates the privacy or security of systems you don't own or have permission to test

## Legal basis (Brazil)

- **Law 14.155/2021** — amends the Brazilian Penal Code regarding unauthorized access to computer devices, with stricter penalties
- **Law 9.296/96** — governs the interception of communications, including data, without judicial authorization

Similar legislation exists in virtually every country (e.g. the Computer Fraud and Abuse Act in the US). Before using any tool in this project, the user is solely responsible for ensuring they are acting within the law.
